Ristorante-La-Trinita

“Authentic Italian food”

We went to a restaurants with a 4.5 Star rating from 304 reviews, by the name of Ristorante-La-Trinita which is an Italian owned, This restaurant has the original Italian look and feel. They serve pizza and some amazing other meals.

I had the margherita la trinita pizza, how can you go to an Italian Restaurant and not have a pizza, I have had many margherita pizza before, but this pizza is just a cut above the rest, The base is thick and the crust is so crispy and so soft when you bite into it. The cheese just pulls off so smoothly when you bite into a slice and the flavour just explodes in your mouth. This is a must have when you want a pizza.

Margherita La Trinita Pizza

Oxtailhot & Heavenly

Then we also had the Oxtailhot & Heavenly, This is meat beefie meat with gravy and mash potatoes.

This was divine the beef was soft like it was melting in you mouth, mixed with the mash the combination on flavour is tangy and spicy at the same time, talk about confusing your taste buds.

The following list is also a must have, these dishes are amazing, so much to choose from.

Mozambik Linksfield

"Seafood Portuguese Vegetarian Friendly"

With its roots firmly set in passion and its flavours unapologetically Afro Porto, the Mozambik restaurant group is fast becoming one of South Africa’s leading dining brands. With a 4.5 star rating we had to try out this place.

Man! Oh Man! we were not dissapointed, lets just say we got there lunch time and left 2 hours before closing. This place caters for clients from all walks of the food chain, if you don't love your meats they do have treats for you.

If you are a lover of sea foods then you must get the 1kg Prawn with Calamari, These prawns a massive and the calarmai is dripped in the amazing sause, the taste will drive your taste buds insane.

The Calamari is prepared in such away then when it goes into ur mouth it melts slowly, giviing you taste buds this pleasure of savouring every moment as the Calamari melts.

1Kg Prawns with Calamari

Catch of the day with Veggies

If you looking for a wild experiance you must have the catch of the day.

This fish is freshly caught from the river and is preapred on open flames and soaked in amazing sauses, garlic, spicy and tangy flavours, with a side of potjie-veggies...sounds so exciting you have to taste it.

The following list is also a must have, these dishes are amazing, so much to choose from.

Thundergun Steakhouse

"Steakhouse Bar Barbecue"

We went to Thundergun SteakHouse, This place is best known for it's mouth-watering steaks, ribs and burgers – arguably the best in town – the Thunder Gun also offers a range of starters and desserts, chicken, fish and other great options.

So with and introduction and statement you know we had to check it out. We went to check it out MAN! wish I had a bigger stomach, or an extra one...LOL!!

Asian Beef Salad

So due to my curiosity I wanted to know what an Asian Beef Salad tasted like. Let's be real you would too. So my Asian salad arrives and I mean it has beef in it, and they ain't small either. The beef is soft perfectly flamed. Foodies the BEEF is flame grilled, so the texture and aroma causes your mouth to water.

I tried my salad and thats the last time i lifted my head or spoke to anyone, till it was done. So after that I wanted more and guess what I ordered...Yes Foodies Flame Grilled

Flamed Grilled Ribs with Hot Spice with a side of Chips, onion rings and a salad.

This Foodies is a meal that requires hands, not a knife and a fork. These ribs are just on another level, the spice, flavours and the smell all of it just stimulates every part of ur taste buds. This meal I rate is my most favourite through out my adventures eating out in Johannesburg.

This South African Foods is a must have, you need to eat it. trust me I am a chef.
